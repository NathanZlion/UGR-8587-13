ABOUT GRADE BOOK

Track Your Grades

Easily keep track of all your grades in one place with our user-friendly interface. You can add, edit, and delete grades for different subjects, and view your overall academic progress over time.

Calculate Your GPA.

Our GPA calculator allows you to quickly and accurately calculate your grade point average and CGPA. You can also view your grades in a graph format to track your progress and identify areas for improvement.

Create Customized Study Plans

Easily create personalized study plans that fit your schedule and learning style. Our platform allows you to add flashcards, notes, and reminders for exams and assignments, so you can stay on top of your academic commitments..

HOW TO USE

  • Learn how to create an account and log in to the website.
  • Understand how to add, edit and delete grades from different subjects.
  • Calculate your GPA and CGPA with our easy to use calculator.
  • Create personalized study plans and flashcards for your exams.
  • Set reminders for assignments, exams, and deadlines to stay on top of your academic schedule.
  • Track your academic progress over time and see where you can improve.
